The recent outbreaks of H1N1, Influenza, Ebola, MERS, Zika, and now SARS-Cov2, which has infected over 36 million people to date worldwide, punctuate the urgency of bringing vaccines to market quickly and efficiently. Ramping up production and shortening development times is crucial. Vaccine development typically takes years. However, pandemics require a paradigm shift in our thinking a better, faster, safer, more collaborative, and flexible approach to manufacturing that will safely speed the time-to-market. Enter Single-Use-Technology (SUT).
Single-Use is advancing vaccine manufacturing, whether it is for attenuated viruses, vectors, or nucleic acid platforms.
Advantages of Single-Use
• Faster production
• Reduced capital expenditures
• Process and scale flexibility
 • Reduced cross-contamination risk
• Ease of use
• Reduced cleaning costs
The webinar, e-book, white papers, and video below provide an abundance of information and best practices. They will teach you about the unique solutions our collaboration with the Jenner Institute and other vaccine thought leaders have yielded and how you too can establish a flexible, scalable, and rapid GMP-compliant SUT process for your own workflow.
